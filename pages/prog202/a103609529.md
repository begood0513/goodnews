  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/波贝市.htm">

  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/火灾.htm">
   火灾
  </ok>
  ，，火势迅速延烧。目前已知约10人丧生、30人受伤。起火原因还有待厘清。

  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/柬埔寨.htm">

  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/火灾.htm">
   火灾
  </ok>
