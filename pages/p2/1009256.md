  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/新疆书记" target="_blank">
   新疆书记

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/中共" target="_blank">
   中共
  </span>

 </p>
 <p>

 </p>
