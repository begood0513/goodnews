 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/中纪委" target="_blank">

 </p>
 <p>

 </p>
 <p>
  消息还说，前中纪委驻国安部纪检组长任爱荣（女）将接替
  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/孙新阳" target="_blank">
   孙新阳
  </span>
