 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/新加坡" target="_blank">
   新加坡

 </p>
 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/销售" target="_blank">
   销售

 <p>

 </p>
 <p>
