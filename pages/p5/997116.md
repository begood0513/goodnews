  </span>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/拜登" target="_blank">
   拜登
  </span>
  总统接纳了这项建议，决定延长4年。

 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/光伏" target="_blank">
   光伏
  </span>
