 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/米哈伊尔･布林" target="_blank">
   米哈伊尔･布林
  </span>

 </p>
 <p>

  <span href="https://www.secretchina.com/news/gb/tag/犹太人" target="_blank">

  </span>

 </p>
 <p>
